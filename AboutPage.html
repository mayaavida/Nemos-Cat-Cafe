About Us

Nemo’s Cat Cafe is named after the cutest and chonkiest cat to ever exist, Mr. Nemo himself. Nemo is a friendly and cuddly kitty who loves sitting on keyboards and scratching our furniture :) He is very curious, and loves to explore and stare at birds out the window.

As Nemo’s humans, we felt like it was unfair that we could bring our dog to lots of places - Bars! Cafes! Parks! But there wasn’t a public place we could bring Nemo along to, leaving him to his own devices at home.

We wanted to create a cozy spot where humans and cats could enjoy a delicious drink, snack, or meal, and spend quality time together. We serve locally roasted coffee from Peace Coffee, and pastries and lunch items from local favorite Alma. We also have feline food! We carry a selection of gourmet fish, locally made treats, and catnip.

Bring along a kitty (or two, or three!) and check out our welcoming space for yourself.
